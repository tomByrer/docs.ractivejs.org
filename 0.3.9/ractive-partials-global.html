Ractive.partials

edit this page

Partials can be made globally available to Ractive instances by adding them to Ractive.partials:

Ractive.partials.rickroll = '<iframe width="420" height="315" src="http://www.youtube.com/embed/dQw4w9WgXcQ" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe>'

Where necessary, partials will be parsed at the point of use, and stored as parsed partials thereafter.

If Ractive encounters a partial mustache, e.g. {{>rickroll}}, it will look for instance-specific partials before looking in Ractive.partials.
